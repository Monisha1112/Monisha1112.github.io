THOR ODINSON

DESCRIPTION

Thor Odinson is the son of Odin the Allfather and Frigga, the king and queen of the Planet Asgard.

Thor is the God of Thunder and the King of Asgard.

He wields the power of the ancient Asgardians to fight evil throughout the Nine Realms and Beyond.

He is one of the strongest Avengers.

WEAPONS


MJOLNIR--> The Hammer. Thor's mighty hammer Mjolnir has immense destructive capabilities and can be summoned to Thor's hand whenever the God of Thunder wishes it.

STORMBREAKER--> The Axe. With Mjolnir destroyed Thor received a new weapon, the axe, Strombreaker. Like Mjolnir, it can help harness his powers, and is also capable of opening the Bifrost.
